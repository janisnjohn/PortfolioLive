Why Am I Different

PAST
I have many years experience as a General Manager in the Automotive Parts distribution industry. As a General Manager I love working with people to motivate them to produce results. I have worked with branch and sales managers to increase sales and restructure departments to run more efficiently. It was a competitive market and the ever-changing world of the web and ecommerce made it even more demanding. And I realized that I needed to evolve in my knowledge and skills to keep up with the ever-changing world and the powerful tool of the world wide web.

PRESENT
I wanted to learn the skills I needed to utilize the web and influence ecommerce and our day to day tasks. It is a powerful tool to make information available and influence consumer buying power. I am currently enrolled in a full-stack bootcamp from University of Arizona and will be certified in January 2018. The skills and languages I have acquired are HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node Js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, P5 Js, and React Js. Through this knowledge I desire to create user-friendly apps that make our lives more efficient and save time and money.
